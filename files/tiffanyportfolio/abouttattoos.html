about portfolio lister.tiffany@gmail.com

Business cards are the main way tattoo artists market themselves at conventions. They are also given out at every appointment. The two artists that I created an identity and business cards for were looking for something that reflected their own individual style. Curt's style is very minimalist, and Tom's very graffiti inspired. I chose a square size as it is more modern and would stand out amongst other cards at a convention.
