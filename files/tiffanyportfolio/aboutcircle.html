about portfolio lister.tiffany@gmail.com

Circle is a passion project of mine which aims to help at-risk youth find access to services. I designed the app to be bright and engaging, youthful and still serious. The app has very clear sections which would help someone in a crisis find help. The information is laid out in a very easy to access format which is familiar to the target audience. The colours were chosen as they are calming and supportive. The UI elements are youthful without being ‘urban’.
