The most inviting combination

The allure of South Goa lies in its blend of culture, lifestyle and natural splendour. Boasting 27km of magnificent white-sand beaches running alongside grand palm forests, South Goa is a destination to behold. However, South Goa is not just a beautiful beach, the location also offers vibrant Indo-Portuguese culture, striking architecture, exotic food, colourful music and a laidback lifestyle.

Its time to relax! Golden Sands will provide you with you're very own piece of paradise with contemporary designs and impeccable functionality to meet your needs.

Laying the Golden foundation

Our philosophy is to create sustainable gated residential communities which exist in harmony with the local environment. Our projects are a combination of modern and contemporary architecture that compliment the prominent Indo-Portuguese style of South Goa. We pride ourselves on our attention to detail and ensure that the architecture and interior of all our spaces is of the utmost quality.

Our residential communities offer the perfect environment to live an extraordinary lifestyle, or even as a smart long-term investment.
