Welcome to the Golden Sands Apartments

When you stay at Golden Sands Apartments, you will experience life surrounded by panoramic beaches and plush greenery in a city known for its culture and beautiful architecture. Our apartments are distinctively Indo-Portuguese in style and are conveniently located just 500 meters (or a 10 min walk) from the beach. From spacious rooms that inspire evenings of relaxation with your loved ones, to modern kitchens that let you prepare meals with panache and style. Every square foot echoes luxury at its best. We are a gated community of 36 fully furnished holiday homes of various sizes. The complex itself enjoys a swimming pool, jacuzzi, backup generators as well as round the clock security.
