DIVISIONS

Girls

The highly talented design team of the Girls Division is dedicated to following evolving trends and filling voids in the market accordingly. Its in-depth knowledge of merchandising, sales, licensing and brand management has enabled the Girls Division to expand at a vast pace in private label programs, loungewear, novelty items, brands and licenses. The team’s immense success has afforded it continuously growing relationships with Canadian and American customers.

Boys

The Young Men and Boys Division has extensive product knowledge, which has been the platform for its many great retailer relationships. From concept to completion, this division has mastered every aspect of the supply chain. It has created programs, such as design, manufacturing and marketing that cater to the specific customer. The success of this division originates from the dedicated team, who work with the common goal of establishing an effective apparel collection. This division recognizes customer needs and translates changing trends to the mass consumer market. It focuses on building both large, private label and branded programs with its retailers.

Ladies

The Ladies Division creates Junior, Missy and Plus Size fashion. From casual to career styles, this division offers knit and woven tops and blouses, as well as knit bottoms, including leggings and skirts. It's staff extensively travels the world to keep current with both the evolving European and U.S. trends. With a clear understanding of the market, the division styles fashion and fashion basic key items and effectively targets competitive customer price points. With vast experience in the field, the division has built a loyal following of major Canadian and American clients. Its continued success has been in providing private label programs that best satisfy the needs of individual customers, making the Ladies Division a key contributor to the growth of JCorp.
